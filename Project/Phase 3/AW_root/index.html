Safety Tips

Work Zone Safety

  1. Slow down
  2. Stay focused
  3. Move over early for lane closures
  4. Keep extra space between your vehicle and the one in front of you.

Winter Weather Safety

  1. Keep your car in good shape - Tune-ups, regular maintenance, good tires, etc
  2. Prepare for an emergency - Keep blankets, flashlights, jumper cables, extra clothes or food in your car
  3. Don't rush - allow plenty of time to arrive at your destination
  4. Slow down
  5. Don't crowd or travel beside a snow plow - this can be extremely dangerous for you and the plow driver

General Safety

  1. Buckle up!
  2. Don't drive distracted.
  3. Follow traffic signals
  4. Don't text and drive
  5. Be patient
  6. Don't drink and drive
  7. Use headlights when needed - they're not just so you can see at night
  8. Share the road
